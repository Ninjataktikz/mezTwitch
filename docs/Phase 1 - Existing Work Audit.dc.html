Audit of all prior mez. work against the Twitch & creator-ecosystem goal. Nothing here is a redesign. Strategy identity work from the earlier project is treated as locked.

01 — finished · preserve as-is

The earlier project produced a complete, unusually disciplined identity — at a standard most Tier 1 orgs never reach. All of it is locked:

  • Positioning — "Mastery is quiet: the first CS athlete built with the patience of a luxury house." Archetype: The Master (Sage × Hero).
  • Name system — public brand mez. (always lowercase); in-game tag mezcs_; "Armez"/"Bhatti" reserved.
  • The mark — rectilinear custom wordmark, one 45° diagonal, Signal square point. Exact SVG construction recovered and copied into this project.
  • Color — Ink #0E0E0E · Chalk #FAFAF8 (broadcast variant #F2F2EF already specified) · Signal #2438E8 at ≤5% of any surface · Signal/Dark #6D7CFF for dark grounds · four warm greys.
  • Type — Neue Haas / Helvetica (400, 700 only), engineering mono for data, tabular numerals.
  • Voice — short declaratives, numbers over adjectives, silence after wins.
  • Rules — ≥60% emptiness, radius zero, no gradients/glows/3D/mascots/neon, one Signal element per surface.
  • mez.gg website — full blueprint, design system, and Next.js repo. Out of scope here; untouched.

04 — one real tension to resolve

The animation guide permits exactly two verbs — snap (120ms) and hold — written for the website. A broadcast needs stingers, alert entrances, and countdown states that a 120ms cut cannot fully express. Recommendation: extend, don't violate — a Phase 8 broadcast-motion addendum built from the same DNA (hard cuts, the point as the only moving element, no easing theatrics). The brand already anticipated broadcast (Chalk has an LED variant); the motion system should too. This tension is also the opportunity: on a platform drowning in neon and glow, a silent, rectilinear, mostly-empty stream will read as the most premium thing on Twitch.
