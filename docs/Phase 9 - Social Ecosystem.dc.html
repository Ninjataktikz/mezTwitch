Phase 9 · Social Ecosystem
July 2, 2026

one system,
five feeds.

Twitch is the arena; everything else is distribution. The test from Phase 3 governs all of it: a clip must be attributable to mez. with the logo cropped out.

01 — youtube (@mezcs2) · the archive
  • Banner (2560×1440, safe 1546×423): chalk field, wordmark left of center, mono tagline. From the kit's banner vocabulary.
  • Thumbnail system: Fable plate (Phase 7, prompt 01) + one lowercase Helvetica 700 headline ("ace on mirage.") + mono elo chip. Never faces-with-arrows, never red circles. A shelf of these must look like one publication.
  • Formats: highlights (60–90s) · the review (8–12 min) · the ledger (monthly numbers recap, 3 min).
  • Titles: voice rules apply — "1v4, inferno." not "INSANE CLUTCH?!?"
02 — tiktok / shorts · the sampler
  • Template (1080×1920): gameplay fills the frame; 96px ink caption bar at top: mono label ("faceit · mirage · 1v3") ; the point sits at bar's right end. No subscribe animations, no emoji strings.
  • Intro: none. Clip starts at the action minus 3 seconds. Branding is the caption bar itself.
  • Outro (last 20 frames): hard cut to ink, wordmark + handle, hold, end. The stinger grammar, reused.
03 — x / instagram · the ledger, published
  • Stat post (2048×2048): chalk or ink field, three mono numbers (elo delta, K/D, HS%), dated, wordmark bottom-left. Losses posted with identical typography — that's the brand doing its work.
  • Match result: "13–7, ancient. +27." — text-first; graphic version only for milestone results.
  • Profile: avatar = the point; banner = kit banner cropped.
04 — discord · the room
  • Server icon = the point on ink. Banner = Fable paper-texture plate + wordmark.
  • Channels lowercase mono style: #announcements → "the ledger", #clips → "the reel". Minor-safety rules from Phase 3 apply doubly here: no DMs open, parent admin.
05 — clip captions & lower thirds (all platforms)
  • One lower-third: ink strip, mono label left ("mez · faceit lvl 10"), point right. Enters with snap, holds, leaves. Used in every edited video — never two styles.
  • Caption voice: map · situation · result. "mirage. 1v3. +25." Numbers close every caption.
06 — cadence (sustainable for a student athlete)

Streams 3×/week max, same slots. One YouTube upload/week. Shorts cut from stream clips — 3/week, batched on one edit day. The ledger posts weekly, automated where possible. Growth comes from consistency and the numbers rising, not volume.
