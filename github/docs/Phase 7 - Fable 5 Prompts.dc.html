The core package is typographic and ships from the Broadcast Kit — no generation needed, and generated text/logos would violate the mark's immutable geometry. Fable 5 produces background plates only; the wordmark and type are composited on top afterward. Every prompt below shares one style contract.

00 — style contract (paste into every prompt)
Minimal editorial photography, documentary realism, available light, warm-neutral palette (off-white #F2F2EF, near-black #0E0E0E, warm greys), high negative space (at least 60% of frame empty), matte finish, no text, no logos, no watermarks, no neon, no glow, no lens flare, no HDR look, no fantasy elements, no people's faces. Composition leaves the [left half / lower third — per asset] completely empty for typography.
01 — youtube thumbnail plate · 2560×1440 (crop 1280×720) · P2

Purpose: background for highlight/review thumbnails; headline + elo chip composited left.

Close-up of a pro-grade gaming mouse on a worn dark mousepad, shallow depth of field, desk lamp side light, dust visible in the light beam, moody but neutral grading. [style contract]. Left 55% of frame is empty dark surface.

Variants: keyboard WASD close-up · headset on stand · monitor glow on a dark room wall (no screen content).

02 — montage title-card plate · 3840×2160 · P2

Purpose: full-bleed plate behind "the ledger — week 12" style title cards in highlight videos.

Empty training facility desk row photographed from a low angle, off-white walls, one chair, morning light through blinds casting hard parallel shadows, architectural stillness. [style contract]. Lower third completely empty.
03 — discord banner plate · 1920×1080 crop 16:9 · P3
Macro photograph of matte paper texture in warm off-white, subtle fiber grain, one hard shadow edge crossing the lower right corner, nearly abstract. [style contract]. Entire left half empty.
04 — x / instagram stat-post plate · 2048×2048 · P3
Overhead photograph of a dark desk surface with a single sheet of off-white paper placed slightly off-center, hard daylight, crisp shadow, documentary flatness. [style contract]. The paper's surface stays empty — numbers are composited onto it.

The setup panel and mez.gg /setup page want REAL documentary photos of his hardware (already specced in the site's content plan: available light, hands and hardware). Fable 5 is the placeholder until those exist — use prompt 01's variants. Real always replaces generated.
