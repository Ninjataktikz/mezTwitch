The identity is locked (Phase 1). This addendum defines only what's new: how mez. behaves as a live creator. One page.

01 — positioning

The stream is not a show. It is an open training session — the public gets to watch a prospect work. This framing solves the 14-year-old problem cleanly: he isn't performing maturity or hype; he's practicing, and practice is age-agnostic. Viewers return for the same reason scouts do: to watch the number go up. The channel's promise: serious CS, measured out loud.

02 — audience, in order
  • 1. FACEIT grinders (16–28) — want to improve; watch for utility, positioning, decision-making.
  • 2. Talent watchers — scouts, org staff, community figures tracking rising players.
  • 3. Peers — young high-elo players. They spread the name.
03 — content pillars
the grind
FACEIT queue sessions. Default content. Title format: "faceit. [elo]. [goal]." — always current numbers.
the review
Demo reviews of his own losses. Rare on Twitch, catnip for improvers, shows maturity no talk can.
the ledger
Weekly numbers, posted, dated: elo delta, K/D, HS%. Wins and losses get identical typography.
04 — on-camera rules (extends the voice)
  • Talk about the game, not about himself. Comms-style narration beats commentary.
  • Silence after wins (already law). After losses: one sentence on what was wrong, then queue.
  • Never engage rage, bait, or age remarks in chat. Mods remove; he plays on.
  • No forced hype, no begging for follows. The follow ask exists once, quietly, in the panels.
05 — minor-safety guardrails (non-negotiable, invisible to viewers)
  • No real name, school, city, or schedule-revealing details on stream, in panels, or in titles. "mez" is the whole identity.
  • Age is stated once (it's part of the prospect story) but never made the content.
  • Whispers off; DMs routed to the business email, which a parent controls. Contact panel says "business inquiries reach management" — true, and it sets a professional frame.
  • Chat: followers-only + unique-chat mode at minimum; a parent or trusted adult holds mod tools every stream; banned-word list before first stream.
  • Webcam frames the player, not the room. Nothing identifying in the background.
06 — what stays consistent, forever

Every surface — Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, Discord, X — is chalk-and-ink with one blue square, lowercase name, mono numbers. A clip should be attributable to mez. with the logo cropped out. That is the test.
