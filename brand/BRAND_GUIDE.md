## The mark
"mez." — rectilinear custom wordmark (zero curves; one 45° diagonal in the z; extended z terminal) + 2.2u square point in Signal. Full construction: Phase 4, Section 2. Clear space 1 x-height; min 64px wide; below that the point flies alone (min 8px).

## Always / Never
ALWAYS: ≥60% emptiness · one Signal element per surface or none · 8-unit module · one-color embroidery test · radius zero.
NEVER: gradients, glows, shadows, 3D, mascots, neon, rounded corners, stretching/outlining/recoloring the mark, a third color on one surface.

## Co-presence rule
Years 1–3 the point never appears without the wordmark on the same surface (exception: platform avatars).
